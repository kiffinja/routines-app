Privacy Policy — Habits

Last updated: April 2026

Data Collection

Habits does not collect any personal data. All your routines and tasks are stored locally on your device.

Data Transmission

No data is transmitted to external servers. The app works fully offline after the first load.

Advertising

The app contains no advertising.

Google Fonts

Fonts are loaded from Google Fonts servers on first launch. See Google's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
